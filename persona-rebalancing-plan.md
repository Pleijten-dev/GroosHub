# Persona Rebalancing Plan

## Executive Summary

**Problem:** 5x usage gap between most used (Ambitieuze Singles: 20) and least used (Zelfstandige Senior: 4) personas.

**Goal:** Balance persona distribution to 10-15 uses per persona.

**Constraint:** Each space must have EXACTLY 5 personas (1:1 swaps only).
